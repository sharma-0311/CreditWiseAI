Credit risk scoring helps evaluate whether a person is likely to repay a loan. Machine Learning models like Random Forest and SHAP improve model performance and interpretability. This method is crucial in reducing the chances of default and ensuring better financial decision-making.

What is a credit score?
A credit score is a numerical representation of a person's creditworthiness.

How to improve credit score?
Timely bill payments, lower credit utilization, and avoiding multiple loan inquiries help improve it.

Why might a loan be denied?
Low income, bad credit score, too many existing loans, or insufficient credit history are common reasons.
